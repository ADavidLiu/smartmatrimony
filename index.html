OUR PROCESS

TARGETED
MATCHES…
MEET TAILORED
MATCHMAKING

We understand you want to meet people that you connect with on a more meaningful level. That is why we worked with experienced psychologists to develop our personality questionnaire which analyses your personality across 9 key attributes. Our matching algorithm then matches you with others based on who you are and what you are looking for. Like magic!

For those who want a more personal touch our team of experienced matchmakers are on hand to help. Your personal matchmaker will spend the time to get to know you and what you are looking for. They will boost your search by searching across our extensive network of members as well as coordinating with other matchmakers to help you find someone special. Let the search begin!
